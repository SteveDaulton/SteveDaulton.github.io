5  Data Types

There are several different data types available to XLISP programmers.

  • lists
  • symbols
  • strings
  • integers
  • characters
  • floats
  • objects
  • arrays
  • streams
  • subrs - [built-in functions]
  • fsubrs - [special forms]
  • closures - [user defined functions]
